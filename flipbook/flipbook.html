Loose Lyrics of Lovely Ladies, 1928

Loose Lyrics of Lovely Ladies is a 1928 song cycle by Holland Robinson, illustrated by Mac Harshberger, that reimagines celebrated historical women as theatrical, modern personas. Each song is inscribed to a contemporary woman, linking these figures to the artists’ social circle. Blending cabaret, jazz-inflected harmony, and Art Deco design, the work captures the cosmopolitan energy and playful experimentation of 1920s Paris and New York.
